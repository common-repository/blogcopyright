Inject a copyright notice into the footer.

== Description ==

Blog Copyright injects a copyright notice into your blog footer. The copyright notice automatically updates the year range as time goes, removing the need to ever revisit your copyright once the plugin is put in place. Adjustments can be made by you, to a variety of options including the starting year and the All Rights Reserved statement. 

[Blog Copyright](http://www.blogtrafficexchange.com/blog-copyright "Blog Copyright") by [Blog Traffic Exchange](http://www.blogtrafficexchange.com/ "Blog Traffic Exchange")

Check out our other [Wordpress Plugins](http://www.blogtrafficexchange.com/wordpress-plugins/ "Wordpress Plugins")

== Installation ==

1. Upload BlogCopyright directory to the `/wp-content/plugins/` directory
2. Activate the plugin through the 'Plugins' menu in WordPress
3. Optionally adjust the options and click 'Update Settings'
